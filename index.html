# Intro

Welcome to my rendition of yet another vaguely academic personal site (that also sort of looks like the rest of them)!

In all seriousness, I’m Cal, a first year physics PhD student at JILA and the University of Colorado Boulder. I currently work on Jun Ye‘s ultracold KRb experiment.

I'm broadly interested in developing improved techniques for control and measurement of ultracold molecules for application to quantum simulation.

Previously I received my A.B. in Physics and S.M. in Applied Mathematics at Harvard in 2020, where I'm grateful to have worked with John Doyle on laser cooling polyatomic molecules for precision measurements of fundamental physics and Ye Tao on ultrasensitive scanning probe microscopy.
